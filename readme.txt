== Description ==

This is the newest, freshest & best wordpress plugin for you in case you want to add a Facebook Like Button to your website / blog! Add a Facebook Like Button to your wordpress blog with a lot of customization options, And you can preview the settings at real-time in the settings section - with our Facebook Like Button Widget.
Add the Facebook Like Button count, or normal by using short cuts [fb_like] [fb_count].

Add Facebook Recommendations Widget to your blog sidebar or to anywhere using this shortcut [fb_rec] within our Plugin.

Mulitple language support for our Facebook Like Button Plugin, you can change the button language to your language in 3.4!

The Facebook like application allows visitors to “like” your page at just a click of the button, it also displays the number of “likes” your page has on Facebook. Every post on your blog will have it's own like button and count. When a visitor likes your post through facebook like button, it appears on her wall and helps spreading the word about your website on facebook. 

Facebook-Like-Button
This plugin adds a Like-Button after every post/page you choose (you can exclude Posts/Pages/..., These excluded sites will have no Like-Button). The Like-Button-Widget includes a little Like-Button-Generator to make it easier for you to get a Like-Button to your sidebar. It also adds a Shortcode for your like-Button. It is also possible to choose the XFBML or the iframe-Button. You could also choose the position of your like-button within the post/page.
